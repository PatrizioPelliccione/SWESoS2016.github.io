Swedish Workshop on the Engineering of Systems of Systems (SWESoS)

SWESoS is the premiere event in Sweden for discussing about Systems of Systems (SoSs).

The event is the place where industry and academia meet to discuss about achievements, challenges, ongoing research and future plan about SoSs. The event is fairly informal, with a focus on presentations and interactions rather than paper publishing. Participation is free of charge.

Proceedings are now available:
SWESoS proceedings.

The workshop was organized in Gothenburg, September 9, 2016, Lindholmen, Jupiter building, room 520 (5th floor).
